About BeeBee AI

BeeBee AI LTD is an international technology company headquartered in New Zealand, with teams distributed across New Zealand, the UK, Canada, the US, and China. The company focuses on GenAI intelligent product development and was founded in 2022 by veterans with over 20 years of Internet/IT experience and 8 years of AI multimodal application development experience.

The company's mission is to enhance personal and organizational productivity through 'AI × Private Knowledge'. Its core product 2Brain, launched in 2024, allows enterprises to import multimodal content (documents, audio/video, websites, chat logs) to form private knowledge bases, visualized in real-time as dynamic knowledge graphs, enabling deployment of RAG-based chatbots and business flow agents within minutes.

2Brain has provided enterprise-level application implementation services to dozens of global clients, redefining how knowledge is applied in various scenarios from Fortune 500 training programs to key customer support assistants.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can my child join Bee Alpha without programming experience? +

Camp A is designed for beginners and provides necessary programming guidance. We recommend learning basic Python before joining to better understand the 2Brain architecture modules. For students with no programming background, we focus on their mathematical and logical abilities during the interview. Camps B and C require some programming foundation, but we assess student levels during interviews to ensure they can keep up with the course.

What equipment and technical environment are needed? +

Camp A only requires a standard development laptop (16GB+ RAM). Camps B and C involve model deployment, so we recommend using a computer with NVIDIA GPU or our provided cloud development environment. We provide necessary API KEYs for LLMs and cloud/local GPU resources for deployment. All necessary software and environment setup guides will be provided before the course starts.

How is 2Brain architecture different from other AI frameworks? +

2Brain is a complete enterprise-level knowledge intelligence system architecture, not just an AI framework. It integrates the complete CODE process from data Collection, knowledge Organization, information Distillation to intelligent Expression, validated in multiple large enterprise projects. Students will learn industry-proven technical architecture, not just theoretical or experimental frameworks.

How does this help with school and college applications? +

Bee Alpha projects serve as important portfolio pieces, especially demonstrating practical abilities in AI and software development. Projects based on 2Brain architecture significantly help applications for Computer Science, Data Science, AI and related majors. We also provide project certificates and mentor recommendation letters, adding extra value to applications.

Classes are scheduled on weekends, only 2 half-days per week, not affecting regular school courses. We offer multiple timezone options for students from different regions. Our 15 global mentors cover New Zealand, UK, US, Canada, China timezones, ensuring students can find suitable class times.
